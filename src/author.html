About

Glenn Mosby

Glenn Mosby is a storyteller, poet, mentor, and entrepreneur from Richmond, Virginia, whose passion for writing began at an early age. Inspired by his mother’s gift for poetry and expression, Glenn developed a love of creative storytelling that would later lead to his first children’s book.

Throughout his life, Glenn has worked in public service, youth mentorship, education programs, and community leadership. His experiences, humor, and imagination come together in Old Farmer John Had a Farm, a playful story that entertains children while celebrating creativity and individuality.
