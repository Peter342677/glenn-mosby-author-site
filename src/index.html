About

Glenn Mosby

Glenn Mosby is a storyteller, poet, mentor, and entrepreneur from Richmond, Virginia, whose passion for writing began at an early age. Inspired by his mother’s gift for poetry and expression, Glenn developed a love of creative storytelling that would later lead to his first children’s book.

Throughout his life, Glenn has worked in public service, youth mentorship, education programs, and community leadership. His experiences, humor, and imagination come together in Old Farmer John Had a Farm, a playful story that entertains children while celebrating creativity and individuality.

The Book

old farmer john had a farm

Old Farmer John Had a Farm is a fun and imaginative children’s book by Glenn Mosby that brings storytelling, humor, and playful farm adventures together in a memorable way.

Inspired by the rhythm of classic nursery rhymes, the story introduces readers to a lovable farmer and a collection of unique animals with amusing personalities and unexpected traits. Each page creates a joyful read-aloud experience that children and families can enjoy together.

With its playful tone and charming repetition, the book celebrates creativity, individuality, and the joy of being different.
